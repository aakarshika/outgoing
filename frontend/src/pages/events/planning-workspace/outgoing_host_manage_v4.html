Vinyl request wall
Pin a board at the entrance where guests write their track requests. The DJ works through them across the night.
🆕
String lights
Warm overhead bulb lights transform any rooftop instantly. Rent them for a few hundred rupees.

At the peak of the night, bring out a cake. No occasion needed. Costs almost nothing, remembered forever.
